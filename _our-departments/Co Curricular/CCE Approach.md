<h4>Character and Citizenship Education (CCE)</h4>
<p>Character and Citizenship Education in our school embodies the spirit
of our founder, <strong>Blessed Nicholas Barré</strong>, as we strive to
nurture every student to realise her full potential and make a difference
in society.</p>
<p>Our CCE framework is built upon two primary domains: Character Development
and Citizenship Education.</p>
<p>The school virtues, the CCE core values (Respect, Responsibility, Resilience,
Integrity, Care and Harmony) and social emotional competencies are explicitly
taught in:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>School Virtues Programme</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>CCE lessons</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Form Teacher Guidance Programme (FTGP)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Grace and Love Programme</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Student Leadership Programme</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Cyber Wellness Programme (CW)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Education and Career Guidance Programme (ECG)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Sexuality Education (SEd)</p>
</li>
</ul>
