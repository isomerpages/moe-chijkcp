<h4>Values - in - Action</h4>
<p>In their journey from Primary 1 to Primary 6, students participate in
diverse experiential learning and community outreach initiatives designed
to foster a deep sense of social responsibility. By applying the Design
Thinking Process to their Values in Action (VIA) projects, students learn
to identify community needs and experience the genuine joy of contributing
to society.</p>
