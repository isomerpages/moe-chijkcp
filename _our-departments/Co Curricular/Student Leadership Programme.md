<h4>Student Leadership Programme</h4>
<p>Anchored on our belief that every girl has the potential to lead, our
school-wide student leadership programme develops self-management and leadership
skills progressively from Primary 1 to 6.</p>
<p>Grounded in the <strong>5 Practices of Exemplary Leadership (5 PELs)</strong>,
students are guided to:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Model the Way</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Inspire a Shared Vision</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Challenge the Process</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Enable Others to Act</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Encourage the Heart</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Student leaders are given opportunities to develop their leadership skills
through training, school-based initiatives and student-led projects. They
learn to take ownership, work with others, promote school values and contribute
meaningfully to the community.</p>
