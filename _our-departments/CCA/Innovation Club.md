<h2>Innovation Club (Modular)</h2>
<p><strong>Level:</strong>&nbsp;P3 to P6
<br><strong>DAY:</strong>&nbsp;Friday
<br><strong>TIME:</strong>&nbsp;2:15pm - 4:00pm
<br><strong>VENUE:</strong>&nbsp;School (Cyberlab &amp; Teaching Lab)
<br><strong>TEACHERS I/C:</strong>&nbsp;Mdm Zhai Yuan (Advisor), Ms Angelia
Zhang, Ms Li Meina &amp; Ms Ng Huimin</p>
<h4>SYNOPSIS:</h4>
<p>The Innovation Club nurtures creative and innovative thinking through
engaging STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering &amp; Math) and Makers
activities. These experiences encourage students to step beyond their comfort
zones as adventurous problem-solvers, while building perseverance and resilience.
Through coding and collaborative projects, students develop a deeper understanding
of technology, celebrate successes, and embrace failures as part of the
learning journey.</p>
