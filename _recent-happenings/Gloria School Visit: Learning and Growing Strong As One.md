<p>From 24 to 26 February 2026, CHIJ (Katong) Primary warmly hosted students
from Surabaya for a meaningful school exchange programme. Through classroom
learning, cultural activities, and shared experiences with their IJ buddies,
students built meaningful connections and learned from one another.</p>
<p>Working alongside their buddies, students engaged in conversations and
collaborative activities that strengthened their communication skills and
confidence. These interactions fostered greater cultural awareness as students
shared about their school life, traditions, and experiences, deepening
their understanding and appreciation of different perspectives.</p>
<p>Through cultural workshops and heritage learning experiences, students
developed a deeper appreciation of Singapore’s multicultural identity while
forming new friendships. The programme provided valuable opportunities
for students to bond, learn together, and grow in global awareness.</p>
<p>This enriching exchange brought students together in the true spirit of
being <em>Strong As One</em>, forging friendships and meaningful memories.</p>
