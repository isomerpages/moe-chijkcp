<p>This term, our After-School Workshops were designed with a clear purpose
— to provide authentic learning experiences while nurturing 21st Century
Competencies.</p>
<p>Our girls took ownership of their learning by:</p>
<p>✔️ Signing up for workshops they were genuinely interested in</p>
<p>✔️ Keeping track of their own schedules</p>
<p>✔️ Managing themselves responsibly after school</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>In Term 1, we saw enthusiastic participation across disciplines:</p>
<p>English Language – Quick Write, Board Games</p>
<p>Science – Bath Bombs</p>
<p>Mathematics – Escape Room</p>
<p>A special first this year — workshops were opened to our Primary 3 girls!</p>
<p>While there were a few scheduling hiccups (great learning moments!), the
girls rose to the challenge with resilience. They were engaged, motivated,
and have grown steadily in confidence and independence.</p>
<p>Seeing our girls step up, try new experiences, and grow in independence
reminds us that authentic learning happens when choice, responsibility
and curiosity come together.</p>
