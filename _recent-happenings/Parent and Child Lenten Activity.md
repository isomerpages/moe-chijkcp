<p>Many Catholic parents and students participated in our annual Parent-Child
Lenten Activity held in the afternoon of Wednesday, 11 February 2026. Together,
they explored this year’s school theme, Strong As One, and reflected on
how Scripture calls us to grow stronger together as the season of Lent
begins next week. The session concluded with a meaningful craft activity
which beautifully captured their shared journey of faith.</p>
