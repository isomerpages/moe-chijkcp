<p>On 16 February, our school celebrated Chinese New Year with great excitement
and festive spirit. Students delighted the audience with lively performances,
showcasing their talents and spreading joyful cheer. The highlight of the
celebration was the energetic lion dance performance by Chung Cheng High
School, which thrilled the students and staff with its vibrant drums and
skillful choreography. Some students and teachers were invited on stage
to participate in interactive games themed around the Year of the Horse,
adding laughter and enthusiasm to the event. The celebration ended with
classroom activities where students made festive handcrafts to take home
as meaningful keepsakes.&nbsp;</p>
