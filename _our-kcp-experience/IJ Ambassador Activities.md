<h2><strong>Celebrating Our Legacy, Championing Our Heritage</strong></h2>
<p>At CHIJ (Katong) Primary, we are proud to belong to the esteemed CHIJ
family, a sisterhood rooted in a rich heritage and enduring traditions.
This legacy is not merely remembered; it is lived daily, shaping the values
we nurture in our students and fostering in them a deep sense of identity
and belonging.</p>
<p>Our girls grow up knowing they are part of something larger than themselves.
Across all CHIJ schools, shared symbols, traditions and experiences unite
generations of IJ girls, creating a bond that is both meaningful and lasting.
It is here that friendships are formed and nurtured; friendships that endure
well beyond the school years, for truly, IJ friends are friends for life.
Through thoughtfully designed programmes and authentic experiences, we
help our students understand and live out the CHIJ spirit so that this
connection becomes personal, purposeful and enduring.</p>
<p>At the heart of a CHIJ (Katong) Primary education is a strong foundation
grounded in the love of God and the teachings of the Catholic Church. Each
girl is reminded that she is deeply loved and this belief shapes her character
and worldview. Guided by Catholic Social Teachings - respect for the dignity
of every person, a commitment to the common good and a responsibility towards
others, our students learn to act with compassion, integrity and purpose.</p>
<p>As Nicolas Barré reminds us, “Educate them well… and they will change
the world.” This conviction continues to guide our mission as we nurture
young women who are not only knowledgeable but also grounded in faith and
values.</p>
<p>We believe that true education goes beyond academic success. It is about
forming young women of character - grounded in truth, guided by justice,
respectful of freedom and driven by love. Together, our students build
a caring and united IJ community, one that seeks to make a meaningful difference.</p>
<p>Our hope is that every CHIJ (Katong) Primary girl leaves our school not
only academically prepared but also values-driven and ready to lead with
conviction, serve with compassion and carry forward the proud legacy of
the CHIJ family as agents of change in the world.</p>
